All over the Grand Hotel as here in the Deluxe Room, truthfulness and authenticity have top priority. Leave the everyday behind for a moment and enjoy the stylish, candlelight ambience. Typical delicious meals with other palatable delights and fine wines grown in the best regions are offered by our service team in the Deluxe Room. Our chef, Sebastian Schuster, selects with his staff, as much as possible, products of the region which means that the origin of all products is known. The Deluxe Room can be easily partitioned from the restaurant area and can be offered as a small seminar room for a maximum of 25 people. The latest technical equipment, e.g. 80” flat screen for presentations, also workshop rooms, can be made available. Please contact us to help organize your meeting.
